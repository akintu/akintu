# help_accuracy_penalties.txt

Some weapons in Akintu are difficult to use without proper training.
Characters without specific training suffer a reduction in their accuracy
when using these weapons.

Katana
  -75% Accuracy
  Classes that don't suffer the penalty: Ninja

Longbow
  -25% Accuracy
  Classes that don't suffer the penalty: Dragoon, Anarchist, Marksman,
  Druid, Tactician, Assassin, Arcane Archer
  
Polearms (Spears and Halberds)
  -30% Accuracy
  Classes that don't suffer the penalty: Barbarian, Dragoon, Weapon Master,
  Spellsword
  
Shuriken
  -75% Accuracy
  Classes that don't suffer the penalty: Ninja
