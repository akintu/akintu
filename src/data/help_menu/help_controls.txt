# help_controls.txt

Overworld Controls

  UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT  --> Movement (Hold to run)
  $BASHCHEST$  --> Bash locked chest
  $CHARSHEETOPEN$  --> Display Character Sheet
  $SHOPOPEN$  --> Open Shop (Towns only) 
  $GETITEM$  --> Get items from chest/Pick locks on chests
  $INVENTORYOPEN$  --> Open Inventory
  $STARTRESPEC$  --> Respec character (set at level 1, use $STARTLEVELUP$ to regain levels)
  $QUIT$  --> Open Save Menu
  $STARTLEVELUP$  --> Manual levelup (only used if you have respecc'd your character)
  
Combat

  $ABILITIESOPEN$  --> Open ability selection
  $ANALYZETARGET$  --> Display strengths/weaknesses of target 
  $ACTIVATESELECTED$  --> Use selected ability/spell/consumable
  $SPELLSOPEN$  --> Select spells from spellbook
  $CYCLETARGETF$  --> Cycle targets
  $CONSUMABLEOPEN$  --> Open consumable selection
  $ENDTURN$  --> End turn
  $SWITCHGEAR$  --> Switch to alternate weapon set
  $SELECTSELF$  --> Select self as a target
  $CYCLETARGETB$  --> Cycle targets in reverse
  
Inventory

  $INVENTORYCLOSE$  --> Close inventory
  $INVENTORYDROP$  --> Destroy an item from your inventory
  $INVENTORYEQUIPMH$  --> Equip weapon/armor from your inventory
  $INVENTORYEQUIPMHALT$  --> Equip weapon/shield in an alternate slot
  $INVENTORYEQUIPOH$  --> Equip weapon/ring in your off-hand
  $INVENTORYEQUIPOHALT$  --> Equip weapon/ring in an alternate off-hand slot
  $INVENTORYUNEQUIP$  --> Unequip equipment from your equipped gear
  
Shop

  $SHOPTRANSACTION$  --> Buy/Sell item
  $SHOPCLOSE$  --> Exit shop

Scrolling (Combat and overworld)

  $SCROLLUP$  --> Scroll up
  $SCROLLDOWN$  --> Scroll down
  $SCROLLTOP$  --> Scroll to top
  $SCROLLBOTTOM$  --> Scroll to bottom
  
Other (Defaults only)

  A or SPACE --> Accept (most menus)
  ESCAPE     --> Close (most menus)
  Vim keys   --> Alternate movement options
  Numpad     --> Alternate movement options
