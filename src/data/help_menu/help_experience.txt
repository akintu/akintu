# help_experience.txt

The details for gaining experience from combat are listed in the Akintu
wiki.  A general experience required to level up chart is listed below:

Level 1  --> 2  : 30 XP
Level 2  --> 3  : 120 XP
Level 3  --> 4  : 420 XP
Level 4  --> 5  : 820 XP
Level 5  --> 6  : 1570 XP*

Maximum Level is 20.

* Level 6+ is coming soon.
